Submitting your listing for publication

Once your app listing meets the {% data variables.product.prodname_marketplace %} requirements, you can submit your app. For an overview of the app listing requirements, see “Requirements for listing an app.”

  1. In the top left of the app listing page, click Overview. For information on finding your app listing page, see “Drafting a listing for your app.”
  1. To submit your your completed app listing, click Request publish.

An onboarding expert will reach out to you with additional information.
