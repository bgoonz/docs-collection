Canceling or editing an invitation to join your organization

Next to the username or email address of the person whose invitation you’d like to edit or cancel, click Edit invitation.

You may need to click View all invitations at the bottom of the window to find the person you’re looking for. 6. Decide whether to edit or cancel the user’s invitation to join your organization. - To edit the user’s invitation, select a different role or team, then click Update invitation. - To cancel the user’s invitation to join your organization, click Cancel invitation.
