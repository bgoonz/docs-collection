Connecting your identity provider to your organization

When you enable SAML SSO for your {% data variables.product.product_name %} organization, you connect your identity provider (IdP) to your organization. For more information, see “Enabling and testing SAML single sign-on for your organization.”

You can find the SAML and SCIM implementation details for your IdP in the IdP’s documentation. - Active Directory Federation Services (AD FS) SAML - Azure Active Directory (Azure AD) SAML and SCIM - Okta SAML and SCIM - OneLogin SAML and SCIM - PingOne SAML - Shibboleth SAML

{% note %}

Note: {% data variables.product.product_name %} supported identity providers for SCIM are Azure AD, Okta, and OneLogin. {% data reusables.scim.enterprise-account-scim %} For more information about SCIM, see “About SCIM.”

{% endnote %}
