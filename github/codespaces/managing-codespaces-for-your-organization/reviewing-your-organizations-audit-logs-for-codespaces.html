When any member of your organization performs an action related to {% data variables.product.prodname_codespaces %}, you can review the actions in the audit log. For information about accessing the log, see “Reviewing your audit log.”

The audit log includes details such as who performed the action, what the action was, and when the action was performed. For information on {% data variables.product.prodname_codespaces %} actions, see “{% data variables.product.prodname_codespaces %} category actions.”
