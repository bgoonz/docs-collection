Submitting your bank information

As a sponsored organization, you will receive payouts to a bank account in a supported region. This can be a dedicated bank account for your organization or a personal bank account. You can get a business bank account through services like Stripe Atlas or join a fiscal host like Open Collective. The person setting up {% data variables.product.prodname_sponsors %} for the organization must live in the same supported region, too. {% data reusables.sponsors.stripe-supported-regions %}
