Finding discussions

  1. Navigate to {% data variables.product.prodname_dotcom_the_website %}.
  2. In the top-right corner of {% data variables.product.prodname_dotcom_the_website %}, click your profile photo, then click Your discussions.
  3. Toggle between Created and Commented to see the discussions you’ve created or participated in.
